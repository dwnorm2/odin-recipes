Matcha Latte

Description

This is the world's best banana smoothie! Whether it's for breakfast, pre/post workout, or dessert, this smooothie will always make you feel amazing. :)

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 cup frozen mixed berries of your choice
  • 1 cup plant-based milk (oat, almond, soy, etc.)
  • 1 cup water

Steps

  1. Peel bananas
  2. Break bananas in half
  3. Put all ingredients into blender
  4. Pulse blender for 30 seconds (or until creamy consistency)
  5. Pour into glass and enjoy :)
