Avocado Toast

Description

Easy, quick, yet delicious avocado toast. :)

Ingredients

  • 3 slices sourdough bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • Everything bagel seasoning

Steps

  1. Toast sourdough bread
  2. Remove skin and pit from avocado
  3. Cut avocado into small pieces
  4. Spread/mash avocado onto toast
  5. Season with everything bagel seaoning to taste
