Iced Matcha Latte

Description

A refreshing and nutritious iced matcha latte

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp matcha powder
  • 1/2 cup plant-based milk (oat, almond, soy, etc.)
  • Ice
  • Water

Steps

  1. Heat up water to 175F
  2. Empty 1tsp matcha powder into bowl
  3. Pour heated water into matcha powder and whisk for 45 secs
  4. Fill empty glass with ice
  5. Pour oat milk and whisked matcha into the glass
  6. Stir and enjoy :)
